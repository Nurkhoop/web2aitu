Tribute to Steve Jobs

1955 – 2011 | Co-founder of Apple Inc.

About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur and inventor. He co-founded Apple in 1976 and helped create products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad. He also played a key role in Pixar Animation Studios.

Key Life Events

  1. 1976 – Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak.
  2. 1984 – Introduced the Macintosh computer.
  3. 1985 – Left Apple and founded NeXT.
  4. 1997 – Returned to Apple and revived the company.
  5. 2007 – Launched the iPhone.

Main Contributions

  • Personal computers (Macintosh, iMac)
  • Mobile devices (iPod, iPhone, iPad)
  • Pixar movies (Toy Story, Finding Nemo)
  • App Store ecosystem
  • Digital music (iTunes, iTunes Store)
  • Modern smartphone design (iPhone)
  • Tablet computing (iPad)
